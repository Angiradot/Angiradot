Our Mission

To proclaim the everlasting gospel of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ to all people in the context of the three angels' messages of Revelation 14:6–12. We seek to lead people to accept Jesus as their personal Savior, nurture and disciple them, and prepare them for His soon return.

At SDA Church Kisumu South, we are committed to reaching our community with the message of hope and wholeness found in Jesus Christ through worship, evangelism, compassionate service, and faithful stewardship.

🌎

Our Vision

To be a vibrant, growing, and spiritually mature church community that reflects the love, grace, and character of Christ. We envision every member actively engaged in ministry, every family rooted in God's Word, and every neighbor touched by our service.

We aspire to be a beacon of light in Kisumu South and beyond — a church that is known for its warm fellowship, powerful worship, and transformative community impact.

What Seventh-day Adventists Believe

Our beliefs are rooted in the Bible and centered on the life, death, and resurrection of Jesus Christ.

📖 The Bible

We believe the Holy Scriptures — Old and New Testaments — are the written Word of God, given by divine inspiration. The Bible is the supreme and authoritative guide for faith, practice, and doctrine.

✝️ Salvation Through Christ

We believe in salvation by grace alone through faith in Jesus Christ. His life of perfect obedience, His atoning death, and His resurrection provide the only means of salvation from sin.

🌟 The Second Coming

We believe in the literal, personal, and visible return of Jesus Christ. This blessed hope is the culminating event of the gospel, and we joyfully anticipate His soon return.

⚝️ The Sabbath

We observe the seventh-day Sabbath (Saturday) as God's holy day of rest and worship, as established at Creation and affirmed in the Ten Commandments. The Sabbath is a gift of joyful communion with God.

🌿 Wholistic Health

We believe our bodies are temples of the Holy Spirit and advocate for healthful living — proper diet, exercise, rest, and the avoidance of harmful substances — as an expression of our faith.

🙏 The Church

The church is a community of believers who confess Jesus Christ as Lord. We are called to fellowship, worship, instruction, ministry, and mutual encouragement as we await Christ's return.

Our Story

The Seventh-day Adventist Church, Kisumu South, is part of the worldwide Seventh-day Adventist Church with over 21 million members across more than 200 countries. Our local congregation is a vibrant fellowship of believers committed to living out the principles of Scripture in our daily lives.

Rooted in the rich heritage of the Adventist movement, our church continues to grow as a place where people from all walks of life can find hope, healing, and belonging. We worship together every Sabbath, serve our community through various outreach programs, and support one another in our walk with Christ.

Whether you are new to faith or have walked with the Lord for years, there is a place for you at SDA Church Kisumu South. We invite you to join our family and experience the transforming power of God's love.
