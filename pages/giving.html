Why We Give

🙏

Tithes

Returning to God a faithful tithe (10% of income) is a biblical principle of stewardship. Tithes support the worldwide mission of the church and the gospel ministry.

💖

Offerings

Freewill offerings support local church programs, community outreach, building projects, and special initiatives that bless our community and further God's work.

🌎

Special Projects

From time to time, we collect for special projects such as evangelism campaigns, disaster relief, education funds, and building improvements.
