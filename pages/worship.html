Sabbath Worship Schedule

Every Saturday — Starting at 9:00 AM

Time Program Description
9:00 – 10:30 AM Sabbath School Interactive Bible study in small groups for all ages
10:30 – 11:00 AM Congregational Singing Praise and worship through joyful congregational hymns
11:00 – 11:15 AM Announcements Church news, upcoming events, and community updates
11:15 – 11:30 AM Singing Special music and hymns in preparation for the sermon
11:30 AM – 12:30 PM Divine Service The main worship service featuring the sermon and prayer
12:30 – 1:00 PM Estate Meetings Local estate fellowship groups meet for discussion
1:00 – 2:00 PM Lunch Break Fellowship meal and community time together
2:00 – 4:00 PM Bible Study In-depth Bible study and spiritual enrichment
4:00 – 6:00 PM Departmental Meetings Ministry departments gather for planning and coordination
6:00 – 6:15 PM Sundowner Closing the Sabbath with prayer and reflection

Weekday Programs

Stay connected throughout the week with fellowship and prayer.

🏠

Monday — Estate Fellowship

5:30 – 6:30 PM

Gather with fellow believers in your estate for prayer, Bible study, and mutual encouragement.

🙏

Wednesday — Midweek Prayer

5:30 – 6:30 PM

A powerful midweek prayer meeting to uplift one another and seek God's guidance for our lives and community.

🌞

Friday — Vespers

5:30 – 6:30 PM

Welcome the Sabbath with a special vespers service featuring songs, Scripture readings, and reflections.
