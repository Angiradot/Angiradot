Plan Your Visit

We would love to welcome you — here is everything you need to know

Home / Plan Your Visit

You Are Welcome Here

Whether you are new to faith, searching for a church home, or just visiting Kisumu, we invite you to join us. Our church family is warm, friendly, and excited to meet you. Come as you are — you will feel right at home.

🕐

Service Times

Our main worship day is Saturday (Sabbath):

  • 9:00 AM — Sabbath School (Bible Study)
  • 10:30 AM — Congregational Singing
  • 11:30 AM — Divine Service (Main Worship)
  • 2:00 PM — Afternoon Bible Study

We recommend arriving by 9:00 AM to enjoy the full experience, but you are welcome to join at any time!

👔

What to Wear

There is no strict dress code — come as you are! Most members dress in smart casual or semi-formal attire for Sabbath services. The most important thing is that you feel comfortable.

We believe that God looks at the heart, not the outward appearance. You will be welcomed warmly regardless of what you wear.

📅

What to Expect

A typical Sabbath service includes:

  • Interactive Bible study in small groups
  • Joyful congregational singing and hymns
  • An inspiring sermon from God's Word
  • Warm fellowship and community time
  • A shared lunch (potluck) after service
👶

Children & Youth

We have age-appropriate programs for children and young people:

  • Beginners (0–3 years): Cradle roll with caring staff
  • Kindergarten (4–6 years): Fun Bible stories and activities
  • Primary (7–12 years): Interactive Sabbath School
  • Youth (13+): Dynamic youth Sabbath School class
📍

Getting Here

We are located in Kisumu South, Kenya. Our greeters will be at the entrance to welcome you and help you find your way around.

Connect With Us

We want to make your first visit memorable! Here is how to connect:

  • Fill out a visitor card when you arrive
  • Meet our greeters and pastors after the service
  • Join us for the fellowship lunch
  • Ask about our Bible study groups and ministries

Contact us if you have any questions before your visit.

We Cannot Wait to Meet You!

Have questions before your visit? Reach out to us anytime. We are here to help make your first experience a great one.
