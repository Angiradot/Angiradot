Our Ministries

Each ministry plays a vital role in our church's mission to share the love of Christ.

👥

Youth Ministry

Empowering young people aged 16–35 to develop a personal relationship with Jesus through dynamic fellowship, community service projects, and relevant Bible study programs. Our youth are the future leaders of the church.

👶

Children's Ministry

Nurturing the faith of our youngest members through age-appropriate Sabbath School classes, Vacation Bible School, and special programs that make learning about Jesus fun and engaging.

💕

Women's Ministry

Supporting women in their spiritual journey through prayer groups, mentorship programs, health seminars, community outreach, and empowerment initiatives that build faith and leadership.

💪

Men's Ministry

Building men of faith through fellowship, accountability groups, community service, and leadership development. We encourage men to be spiritual leaders in their homes and communities.

🎶

Music Ministry

Using the gift of music to glorify God and uplift our congregation through our church choir, praise teams, and instrumentalists. We welcome all who wish to share their musical talents.

🩹

Health Ministry

Promoting wholistic health through health expos, cooking classes, fitness programs, and health education. We believe that caring for our bodies is part of our worship to God.

📚

Education Ministry

Promoting Adventist education and supporting students at all levels. We advocate for academic excellence and spiritual development through scholarships and mentorship programs.

🌐

Community Services

Reaching out to our community with compassion through food drives, clothing donations, disaster relief, and partnerships with local organizations to serve those in need.

👪

Family Ministry

Strengthening families through marriage enrichment seminars, parenting workshops, and family counseling support. We believe strong families build a strong church and community.

📰

Communication

Keeping our church connected through digital platforms, social media, the church website, and printed materials. We share stories of faith and keep members informed of church activities.

💰

Stewardship

Teaching biblical principles of stewardship — faithful management of time, talents, and resources. We encourage generous giving and responsible financial management for God's glory.

⏳

Pathfinders & Adventurers

A dynamic program for children and teens that combines outdoor activities, community service, and spiritual development. Pathfinders learn leadership skills while growing in faith.

⚖ PARL — Public Affairs & Religious Liberty

Core Mandate: Promoting and defending religious freedom for all people, ensuring that every individual can worship according to the dictates of their conscience without coercion or discrimination.

Key Activities

  • Advocate for freedom of worship and conscience in all spheres of society
  • Engage with community leaders, government institutions, and educational bodies on religious liberty issues
  • Address cases of coercion of students who observe the Sabbath, ensuring their rights are protected in schools and universities
  • Educate church members and the public on religious liberty principles and human rights
  • Build relationships with other faith communities and civil society organizations to promote tolerance and mutual respect
  • Respond to cases of religious discrimination and provide support to affected individuals
EE
Elder Evans PARL Director — SDA Church Kisumu South
