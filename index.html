Our Church Family

Building a community rooted in God's Word, united in love, and committed to service.

📜

Our Mission

To proclaim the everlasting gospel of Jesus Christ to all people, making disciples and preparing the world for Christ's soon return, through worship, witness, and service.

🌎

Our Vision

To be a vibrant, growing church community in Kisumu that reflects the love and character of Christ, reaching every person with the Adventist message of hope and wholeness.

🤝

Our Values

We believe in the authority of Scripture, the gift of the Sabbath, healthful living, and serving our neighbors with compassion and integrity as we await Christ's return.
